DSmeet: 2005 - 2007

DSmeet was an online social network for Nintendo DS matchmaking. It originated in December 2005 and featured user profiles, a friend code sharing system, and custom forums written in vanilla PHP and eventually ported to Zend Framework.

The website spawned StrategyWiki.org, a wiki focused on video game strategy guides. It was then followed up by a more broadly focused gaming social networking website, Abxy.org, which was later featured by Nintendo in their E3 press event.
